1. Research Assistant (Internship)

Responsibility:

While a research assistant is expected to contribute in many ways to the research project, typical responsibilities include the following:

  • Conduct literature reviews
  • Collect and analyze data
  • Provide ready access to all experimental data for the supervisor
  • Attend project meetings
  • Attend area seminars and other meetings as necessary
  • Summarize project results
  • Prepare other articles, reports, and presentations
  • Monitor the project budget

Requirements:

  • Research experience, majors in marketing, advertising, and neurofeedback training are preferred
  • Experience with Microsoft Office, Excel, and PowerPoint
  • Satisfactory academic progress
