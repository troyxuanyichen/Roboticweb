Company Overview

BrainRobotics leverages machine learning to produce intelligent prosthesis at a low-cost basis. BrainRobotics focuses on developing EMG (aka Electromyography) controlled robotic prosthesis upper limbs, which can be directly controlled to perform lots of gestures using muscle signal without significant surgery.
